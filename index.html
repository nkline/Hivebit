What is HiveBit?

What would it take for designers and developers working in closed-source companies and agencies to share their work? We think we found the answer.

HiveBit is a new online community for agency and in-house design, development and marketing professionals. We're creating a place for agencies and companies to display their work through the individuals that built it.
